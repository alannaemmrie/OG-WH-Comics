How to Order

Please inquire about pricing via email. Please indicate the title, issue number and grade of the items that interest you. In the event of multiple inquiries for the same item, I will use the first email received. Postage and shipping are extra and are computed depending on the size and value of the order.

When I receive an order, I will set your items aside and notify you of the total due including postage costs. Payment should be received within a maximum of 7 days from that notification. If payment is not received within this time, the items will no longer be reserved for you.

Additionally, I am more than happy to bring any items to a show for viewing. Please let me know at least two days prior so I can include it with my inventory.
